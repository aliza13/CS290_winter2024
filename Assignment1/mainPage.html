  1. Items you need to get started with crafting potions
    • Brewing Stand - 3 cobblestone blocks, one blazing rod
    • Water Bottle - glass and water
  2. Items able to go in potions
    • Magma cream
    • Puffer fish

Potions can only have one of the following

Glow Stone
- can add to potion to make it stronger
Red Stone
- can add to potion to make it longer
